WHO WE ARE?

T-Handlers Logistics was founded in 2006 with a single truck and a simple mission: to provide reliable transportation services to local businesses. Since then, we have grown into a trusted logistics partner serving over 600 businesses across the region.

We specialize in freight transportation, local delivery, temperature-controlled shipping, and supply chain solutions for businesses of all sizes. Our clients come from various sectors including retail, manufacturing, agriculture, and pharmaceuticals.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner for companies across various industries. We treat every shipment as if it were our own.
